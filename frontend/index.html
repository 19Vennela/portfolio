about

A little about the one
behind the curtain

I'm Vennela — a computer science graduate from MS Ramaiah Institute of Technology. Somewhere between writing my first line of code and running my first usability study, I realised the problems I cared about most were the messy ones — the ones that lived between people and products, strategy and execution, what users want and what businesses can build.

I've sat in founders' rooms at Wholsum, designed for 88,000 employees at Ericsson, grown a 240-member community from scratch, and co-authored an IEEE paper along the way. Different rooms, same instinct: get curious, take ownership, find the thread that makes everything click.

What I'm chasing is that quiet moment when someone pauses and thinks "how did you know exactly what I needed?" Whether that's through product, research, strategy or design — I don't really mind which chair I sit in.
